Terms and definitions

Unless otherwise directly specified in this document, all terms starting from a capital letter have the following meanings for the purposes of this document:

Conference—event, organized and hosted by the Organizer for web-developers and other persons interested in web-development (pitercss_conf) dedicated to discussion of new tendencies, trends and technologies in the field of web-development that will be held in Saint Petersburg at the following address: St. Petersburg, Tihoretski Prospect, 17, (Benua Farm restaraunt), on June 16th, 2017, from 10:00 until 18:00, Moscow time.

Application—request for participation in the Conference, issued and sent to the Moderator through the use of the Site interface.

Participation—access to the venue of the Conference granted to the participant on condition of payment, possibility of communication with other participants of the Conference and attendance of presentations held by the speakers.

Site—the Organizer’s website posted on the Internet at pitercss.com.

Participant—legally capable person aged eighteen or above who has submitted the Application via the Site and paid the Participation fee, or a legally capable person, for whom the Application has been submitted and/or paid by a legal entity which is the employer of such person.

Parties—joint name of the Organizer and the Participant.

1. Subject of the Agreement

1.1. The Organizer provides informational services to the Participants, which consist in provision of the access to the Conference venue and Participation in the Conference to the Participant.

1.2. The Participant pays for the services provided by the Organizer in the manner provided by Section 3 of this Agreement.

1.3. The Agreement shall be deemed concluded from the moment of payment of Participation fee by the Participant.

2. Conclusion of the agreement

2.1. In order to conclude the agreement, the Participant shall fill in the Application on the Site and pay the Participation fee in accordance with the price specified on the Site at the moment of payment.

2.2. After payment of the Participation fee the Participant received the notification on approval of Participation fee payment to the e-mail specified by the Participant in the Application.

2.3. The Participant shall undergo registration in order to access the Conference venue on the date of the Conference.

3. Cost of services and payment procedure

3.1. Information on the Participation fee is posted on the site.

3.2. Cost of the Participation shall not include: cost of travel to the Conference venue, cost of food, cost of any possible handouts offered during the conference as well as any other benefits other than Participation of the Participant in the Conference.

3.3. Payment of the Participation fee is performed on the basis of 100% prepayment.

3.4. If the Participant shall pay the Participation fee himself, the payment is made directly via the site, with the help of the following payment systems:

  • Yandex.Kassa
  • PayPal

3.5. If the legal entity (employer) shall pay the Participation fee for the Participant, the payment is made in non-cash form, by bank transfer to the Organizer’s banking account on the basis of the invoice issued by the Organizer to such entity. The invoice is issued and sent to the legal entity on the basis of the input data to the specified e-mail address.

3.6. The Participant is deemed to have fulfilled his obligations on payment of services from the moment the funds are transferred to the banking account of the Organizer in the established amount in case of non-cash payment, or the information on the payment made by the Participant provided by the payment system operator.

3.7. All payments under the agreement are made in Russian Rubles.

4. Withdrawal

4.1. The Participant has the right to withdraw from participation in the Conference within 7 (seven) calendar days before the date of the Conference by notifying the Organizer in writing via e-mail to the e-mail address specified in section 10 of the Agreement.

4.2. If the notification described in the paragraph 4.1 above is received within the term specified in the same paragraph, the Organizer returns the Participation fee to the Participant to the same e-wallet (account) in the same e-payment system which was used by the Participant to pay the Participation fee. In case of technical impossibility to return the Participation fee to the e-purse (account) which was used by the Participant to pay the Participation fee, the repayment of funds shall be carried out in accordance with banking details of the Participant, which in case of impossibility of the return payment will be requested from the Participant by the Organizer by means of the relevant request to the Participant’s e-mail address specified in the Application.

4.3. If the Participant shall withdraw from Participation less than 7 (seven) calendar days before the date of the Conference, or if the Participant fails to attend (participate in) the Conference on the date of the conference, the funds paid by the Participants for Participation in the Conference shall not be repaid or reimbursed to the Participant.

4.4. If the payment for the Participant is made by employer (or other legal entity), the funds are returned to the banking account of the legal entity on the basis of application for return.

5.3. The participant is also not responsible for effectiveness of the information received by the Participant during Conference for the Participant.

6.1. The Agreement is valid until the Parties fulfill their mutual obligations.

6.2. The Agreement can be subject to early termination in the manner prescribed by the current legislation and Agreement, taking into account provisions of paragraphs 4.2 and 4.3 of the Agreement.
